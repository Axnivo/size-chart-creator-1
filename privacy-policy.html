Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Collections Creator ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

2. Information We Collect

When you install and use Collections Creator, we collect the following information:

  • Shop Information: Your Shopify store domain, store name, and Shopify store ID
  • Product Information: Access to product data solely for the purpose of organizing them into collections
  • Collection Data: Information about collections you create, including collection names and descriptions
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens necessary to authenticate your store and perform authorized actions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively for:

  • Creating and managing collections in your Shopify store
  • Authenticating your store and maintaining secure sessions
  • Providing customer support and responding to your requests
  • Improving our app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmissions are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Authentication tokens are securely stored and encrypted
  • We do not store credit card or payment information
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:

  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Transfer your data outside of the Shopify ecosystem without your consent

We may disclose your information only:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Authentication tokens are maintained while your app installation is active
  • Upon app uninstallation, we delete all associated data within 30 days
  • You may request data deletion at any time by contacting us

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

8. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • We process data based on legitimate interests and consent
  • We respond to data subject requests within 30 days
  • We maintain records of processing activities
  • We implement privacy by design principles

9. CCPA Compliance

For California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • You have the right to know what personal information we collect
  • You have the right to request deletion of your personal information
  • You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • You have the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

10. Cookies and Tracking

Collections Creator does not use cookies or tracking technologies for analytics or advertising purposes. We only use essential session management provided by Shopify's infrastructure.

11. Third-Party Services

Our app operates within the Shopify platform and complies with Shopify's Privacy Policy. We do not integrate with any third-party services beyond what is necessary for Shopify app functionality.

12. Children's Privacy

Collections Creator is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
